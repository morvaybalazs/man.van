Our Tailored Moving Options

Depending on your specific requirements and budget, we offer a variety of moving services to choose from. The first three options operate on an hourly rate, while the fourth is a reasonable set price dependent on the job requirements. For exact prices, please get in touch with us.

You Load We Drive

This is our most cost-effective option where you can hire us just to drive and you load the van yourself. Ideal for those who want to save money and don't mind the heavy lifting.

Man With a Van

Our driver will assist with carrying and loading items. This option is ideal for jobs that require only one person, such as small single item deliveries, boxes, and bags.

Let our two professional movers handle all the lifting and hard work while you relax. This option includes one or two vans, as needed.

F.A.Q

  • Is there any additional cost for dismantling or assembling items?

    Yes, dismantling or assembling items does come at an additional cost. We recommend checking with us first before making any arrangements.

  • What information do I need to provide for larger items?

    For larger items, we may ask for pictures or dimensions to ensure they can fit into our vans. Please have this information ready to provide a smooth service.

  • How should I prepare my items for pickup?

    Please be prepared by having your items packed and organized. This helps ensure a swift and efficient pickup process.

  • What about large items that need to be fitted into my home?

    It's important to measure large items to ensure we can fit them into our vans and your home. Please take into consideration doors, hallways, and narrow spaces.

  • What if I am purchasing items from third parties?

    If you are purchasing items from third parties, ensure they are ready to hand over the item to our driver. This will help avoid any unnecessary delays.

  • What is your charging policy?

    Our minimum charge is for one hour. After the first hour, we charge for every 15 minutes, so you wonâ€™t get charged for a whole other hour if we begin a new hour.

  • When does the charging time start?

    The clock starts from the moment of meeting/pickup. This ensures a fair and transparent charging policy.

  • What happens if the item does not fit?

    If the item does not fit and we were not asked to bring tools or it was not agreed upon previously, we will leave the item outside or as near to the drop off as we can.

  • How can I make my move more affordable?

    Making your move more affordable is all about preparation! Start by getting your items ready for the move. If you're able to give us a hand with loading, that's a big plus! By cutting down on the time we need to pack and load, you can save quite a bit on the overall cost of your move. Every little bit helps!
